# Client Scenario

## Client Information

Name: Michael Anderson

Position: Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Company: Nimbus AI

Industry: Artificial Intelligence

Company Size: 120 Employees

Funding Stage: Series A

Funding Goal: $10 Million

## Background

Nimbus AI is preparing to raise $10 million in Series A funding from institutional investors.

Prior to investor meetings and due diligence activities, the CEO wishes to understand what information about him and the company is publicly accessible online.

The concern is that attackers, competitors, journalists, or malicious actors may use publicly available information to conduct social engineering attacks, impersonation attempts, phishing campaigns, or other privacy-related attacks.

## Engagement Objectives

The client has requested a privacy assessment to answer the following questions:

- What information about the CEO is publicly available?
- What could an attacker learn?
- What information could be used for social engineering?
- What privacy risks currently exist?
- What actions should be taken to reduce exposure?

## Assessment Scope

The assessment includes:

- LinkedIn profile
- X/Twitter account
- Instagram account
- Company website biography
- Conference appearances
- Podcast interviews

## Expected Outcome

Provide a structured privacy report containing findings, risk ratings, and recommendations for reducing digital exposure before the funding round.
